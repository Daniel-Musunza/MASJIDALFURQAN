About Us

We are located on Abdel Nasser Rd, Mombasa
We have 2 sections of the mosque: One for men and one for women so everyone is welcome
We were founded in 1990 and we finished constructing our mosque in 1995
At Alfurqan mosque, our story is one of faith, community, and service. Founded with a vision of creating a welcoming space for spiritual growth and collective unity, we have evolved into a vibrant center that enriches lives through prayer, education, and compassionate outreach. Guided by the principles of Islam, we are dedicated to fostering an environment where all individuals, regardless of background, find a sense of belonging.
